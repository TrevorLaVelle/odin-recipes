Description

This simple pasta recipe is a quick and easy meal that's perfect for busy weeknights. It features al dente pasta tossed in a flavorful tomato sauce, topped with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese.

The tomato sauce is made with ripe tomatoes, garlic, and olive oil, simmered to perfection. The pasta is cooked until just tender and then combined with the sauce for a delicious and satisfying dish.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and saut√© until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes to the skillet and cook for 10-15 minutes, until the tomatoes have broken down and the sauce has thickened. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to combine with the sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to heat through.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in the fresh basil.
  6. Serve the pasta topped with grated Parmesan cheese.
